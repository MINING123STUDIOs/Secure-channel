🔐 Secure Channel Assistant ⚡

🧭 How it works

1️⃣ Generate your keys. This creates two keys: a 🔓 public key you can share freely, and a 🔐 private key that must stay on this device.

2️⃣ Share only your public key. Send it to the person you want to talk to — over email, chat, whatever. It's safe even if someone else sees it.

🚫 Never share your private key — with anyone, ever. Not your contact, not "support," not a website asking you to "verify" it. Anyone who has it can read your messages and pretend to be you.

3️⃣ Paste their public key into the "Shared Secret" box and create the shared secret. Both of you do this with each other's public key — this step must be done once per conversation.

4️⃣ Encrypt and send. Type a message, hit Encrypt, and copy the scrambled result to your contact through any channel you like.

5️⃣ Decrypt what you receive. Paste what they send you into the Decrypt box to read it.

⚠️ This tool doesn't send messages for you — it only locks and unlocks them. You're responsible for copying the encrypted text to your contact yourself (email, text, chat — anything works, since it's already unreadable to anyone but them).

🔄 Refreshing or closing this page erases your keys and session. There's no account, no cloud backup, no recovery — if you need to keep talking, keep the tab open.
